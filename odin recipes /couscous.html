what's Couscous

Couscous is a type of food that consists of tiny, granular semolina wheat grains, which are often used as a staple in North African cuisine.
It is a versatile and quick-cooking ingredient that can be prepared in various ways.
Couscous is popular in countries like Morocco, Tunisia, Algeria, and other North African regions.
